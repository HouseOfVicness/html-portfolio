Vic's Flicks

My go-to movies when people ask for my favourites....

The Thing (1982)

If I had to choose only one film to watch for the rest of my life, I would choose John Carpenter's remake of The Thing (Original title: The Thing From Another World). It has a simple premise, a claustrophobic setting and the practical effects still hold up. Not to mention the cast are fantastic. Everyone should see this at least once.

Aliens (1986)

One of the best sci-fi films ever made, one of the best action films ever made and one of the best sequels ever made. Terrifying and fun with amazing effects, models, sets and costumes.

Snatch (2000)

A favourite of me and my family. Super funny with memorable characters and a quotable script. Guy Ritchie at his best.
